Hey my name is Jonathan!

Hey there! I'm Jonathan M. Lindstrom, a student at Carnegie Mellon University studying Information Systems and Human-Computer Interaction. I'm also a member of the university's Swim and Dive Team, where I've been fortunate to earn honors like CSCAA All-American and UAA All-Academic recognition. In my studies, I've really gotten into human-centered design, wireframing, and programming languages like HTML5, CSS3, and Python. I've had some cool experiences too, like being a Teaching Assistant for Database Design & Development, where I helped students grasp SQL fundamentals and safe data management practices. Last summer, I worked as a UI/UX Research Assistant at UChicago's Actuated Experiences Lab, where I got to dive into an HCI project and even co-authored a paper for submission to TEI. Outside of academics, I'm all about chess, diving, yoga, and exploring national parks. You can find more about me and my projects at www.lindstromhci.com.
